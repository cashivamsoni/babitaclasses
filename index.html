What is Babita Classes?

Babita Classes is a non-profit educational institute founded by Babita Soni in 2020, located in Kanpur. It provides free education and activities for underprivileged children.

How can I contact Babita Classes?

You can use the contact form, WhatsApp, or call directly at +91 7388311148.

What services do you provide?

We provide free coaching, stationery, and organize cultural, mental, and physical activities for students.

Where are they located?

Babita Classes is located at 1/2, Juhi Bamburahiya Colony, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh - Pincode-208014, India.

What are the fees and minimum age requirement for getting admission?

Babita Classes is a free-of-cost philanthropic organization for underprivileged children. So, there is no fee. The minimum age required for admission is 5 years. You can admit your child by filling the Admission Form. You can navigate to the Admission Form by the All URLs Section from the menu.

Does Babita Classes provide any online classes?

Currently, we don't provide any online classes but we'll do that in future.

Our Mission

  • At Babita Classes, a philanthropic educational institution, we have embarked on an initiative to provide free classes to underprivileged children, nurturing their academic growth and potential.

  • Our long-term vision is to expand our reach and establish a network of NGOs across India, dedicated to providing educational support and opportunities to disadvantaged children nationwide.

  • We are committed to fostering a supportive learning environment, providing not only quality education but also complimentary stationery items to facilitate their academic pursuits.

  • We look forward to collaborating with like-minded individuals and organizations who share our passion for education and community development.

  • With dedication and perseverance, we strive to make a meaningful impact in the lives of our students and contribute to the betterment of our community.

Why Choose Babita Classes for Underprivileged Children & How Babita Classes Works?

  • Engaging Young Minds at Babita Classes At Babita Classes, we employ a multifaceted approach to engage children in the learning process. Our educational methodology extends beyond traditional teaching methods, incorporating captivating videos and audio materials to convey complex concepts in an accessible and enjoyable manner. This innovative approach not only fosters a deeper understanding of the subject matter but also instills a sense of curiosity and enthusiasm for learning among our students.

  • Why Choose Babita Classes for Underprivileged Children? Babita Classes stands out as an ideal choice for underprivileged children due to its commitment to providing high-quality education free of charge. As a non-profit organization (NPO), we eliminate the financial burden associated with education, refraining from levying admission fees or any other charges. Our aim is to create a stress-free learning environment, allowing our students to focus on their academic growth and development. For further details, please refer to our Terms and Conditions.

  • Physical Education at Babita Classes At Babita Classes, we recognize the importance of physical education in a child's overall development. To this end, we have incorporated physical training (PT) and exercises into our schedule, which takes place every Saturday. Our faculty members guide the students through a range of physical activities designed to promote their physical well-being and teamwork skills. Additionally, our regular tests and assessments serve as mental exercises, helping our students develop their critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Our Services

  • Babita Classes is a philanthropic educational institution dedicated to providing academic support to underprivileged children. Our pedagogical approach encompasses a bilingual curriculum, utilizing both NCERT English and Hindi medium textbooks, as well as board-approved materials. Our educational programs cater to students in kindergarten through 10th grade, fostering a comprehensive learning environment that empowers young minds to achieve their full potential.

From Director's Desk

  • According to Babita Classes founder Mrs. Babita Soni, every child must be educated whether he/she is rich or poor. At Babita Classes, we teach poor children for free. We also provide them with stationery items for absolutely no cost. This empowers their capability to study.

  • Everyone must do something for these children. This doesn't mean that you must provide free education and stationery items to every poor child; rather, you should do so for some children, based on your own capabilities. This will do nothing but improve the future of our nation and society.

  • At Babita Classes, we provide free education and stationery items to more than 35 students. We regularly do functions to improve children's interest in their studies. We also do physical activities to ensure that everyone is physically fit.

  • My vision is that every child gets quality education without any compromise, as this will not only improve his/her future but also improve the future of our society and nation. I hope that you all will support me and do whatever is needed for the poor children.

    • -Babita Soni, Founder - Babita Classes
